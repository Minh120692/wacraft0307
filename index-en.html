Our Solutions

What We Offer

From routine daily tasks to specialized assignments, we provide tailored solutions to meet your needs. By combining Japanese-speaking Vietnamese professionals with AI, we deliver both precision and speed to support your company's DX and growth.

BPO Service

We provide comprehensive support for daily back-office tasks, including data entry, customer management, and list maintenance. This significantly reduces your workload, creating an environment where your team can focus on core business activities.

Sales Support

From creating highly targeted prospect lists to appointment setting and email marketing, we streamline sales activities. We help build systems that directly drive conversions and improve close rates.

AI Utilization Support

We will drive your company's DX (Digital Transformation) through AI tool implementation support and business process automation. We will design the optimal division of roles between humans and AI and build workflows that maximize productivity.

Recruitment Support

We handle recruitment back-office work end-to-end, from candidate sourcing and screening to interview scheduling. By offloading administrative tasks, we speed up and improve the quality of your hiring process.

Team Building

Leverage Japanese-capable Vietnamese talent to build your "extended team." Start small with a single member or a few hours and scale up flexibly as your company grows.

Data Analysis and Organization

Combining AI tools with human judgment, we efficiently perform large-scale data entry, list preparation, and information structuring. We provide accurate data foundations that support fast, informed decision-making.
